# Kubernetes & CNCF Newsletters

A guide to the newsletters worth subscribing to — and the ones that no longer exist.

---

## Active Newsletters

### LWKD — Last Week in Kubernetes Development

- **URL:** [lwkd.info](https://lwkd.info)
- **Cadence:** Weekly
- **What it covers:** A concise digest of upstream Kubernetes development activity — merged PRs, KEP updates, SIG activity, and notable discussions.
- **Why subscribe:** Best signal-to-noise ratio in the ecosystem. If you only read one thing per week, make it LWKD.
- **Format:** Web page (also available via RSS)

### Wisdom of the Cloud

- **URL:** [cncf.io/newsletter](https://cncf.io/newsletter)
- **Cadence:** Monthly
- **What it covers:** The CNCF's official newsletter. Covers the full CNCF ecosystem — project updates, event announcements, community highlights, and ecosystem news.
- **Why subscribe:** Replaced KubeWeekly as the CNCF's newsletter. Broader scope (not just Kubernetes) but lower frequency.
- **Note:** This is a monthly newsletter, not weekly. For weekly coverage, use LWKD.

### KubeWeekly (Archive)

- **URL:** [kubeweekly.io](https://kubeweekly.io) (archive only)
- **Cadence:** Was weekly
- **Status:** **Dead.** Last issue #434, May 2025.
- **What happened:** CNCF discontinued KubeWeekly and launched "Wisdom of the Cloud" as a monthly replacement.
- **Note:** Many staying-current guides still recommend KubeWeekly. It no longer publishes.

## How They Compare

| Newsletter | Cadence | Scope | Status |
|-----------|---------|-------|--------|
| **LWKD** | Weekly | Kubernetes upstream development | Active |
| **Wisdom of the Cloud** | Monthly | Full CNCF ecosystem | Active |
| **KubeWeekly** | Was weekly | Kubernetes ecosystem | Dead (May 2025) |

---

## Recommendation

Subscribe to both active newsletters:

1. **LWKD** for weekly developer-level detail
2. **Wisdom of the Cloud** for monthly ecosystem overview

Together they take less than 30 minutes per month to read and cover both depth (LWKD) and breadth (Wisdom of the Cloud).
