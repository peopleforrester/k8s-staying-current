### Fork Around and Find Out (FAFO)

- **URL:** Available on major podcast platforms (search "Fork Around and Find Out")
- **Formerly:** "Ship It" (The Changelog network)
- **Format:** Discussions about infrastructure, deployment, and operations
- **What happened:** "Ship It" rebranded in 2025 with new hosts and format. If you were subscribed to "Ship It," your feed should have updated automatically.
- **Why listen:** Covers the operational side of cloud native infrastructure.

## Other Notable Podcasts

### The Changelog

- **URL:** [changelog.com/podcast](https://changelog.com/podcast)
- **Scope:** Broader than Kubernetes — covers all of open source
- **Why it's relevant:** Occasionally features Kubernetes and CNCF topics. The network that formerly produced "Ship It."

### Cloud Native Live (CNCF)

- **URL:** [youtube.com/@caborncf](https://www.youtube.com/@caborncf) (livestreams)
- **Format:** Live sessions with project maintainers, webinars, and demos
- **Why it's relevant:** Not a traditional podcast, but CNCF's live content often features the same depth of discussion.

## Listening Strategy

Podcasts are best as a supplement, not a primary source. They provide context and perspective that written sources don't — but they're slower to consume.

**Recommendation:**
- Pick a podcast (FAFO or Cloud Native Live) and listen during commutes
- Don't feel obligated to listen to every episode — scan titles and pick what's relevant to your role
- Use podcasts for the "why" and "context" — use LWKD and the blog for the "what"
