# SIG Contributor Experience (ContribEx)

The SIG dedicated to making the Kubernetes contributor experience better — from first issue to long-term maintainer.

---

## What SIG ContribEx Does

SIG Contributor Experience (ContribEx) builds and maintains the infrastructure that helps people contribute to Kubernetes:

- **New Contributor Orientation** — Monthly live sessions for newcomers
- **Good First Issues** — Curating and maintaining the good-first-issue pipeline
- **Contributor Playground** — The practice repository for learning the PR workflow
- **Onboarding Course** — Self-paced new contributor course
- **Community Connectors** — Helping people find the right SIG, the right issue, the right person to talk to
- **Contributor Documentation** — The contributor guide at kubernetes.dev
- **Community Health** — Surveys, metrics, and initiatives to keep the contributor community healthy

## What ContribEx Can Help With

- **"I want to contribute but don't know where to start"** — They'll point you to the right SIG, issue, or program.
- **"I tried to contribute but got stuck"** — They help unblock contributors who hit obstacles in the process.
- **"I want to improve the contributor experience"** — ContribEx itself welcomes contributions to its own tools and processes.
- **"I want to help other newcomers"** — Mentoring and orientation facilitation are always needed.
