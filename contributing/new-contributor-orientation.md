# New Contributor Orientation

Two paths into the Kubernetes contributor community: a live monthly session and a self-paced online course.

---

## Monthly New Contributor Orientation

**Schedule:** 3rd Tuesday of every month
**URL:** [kubernetes.dev/docs/orientation/](https://kubernetes.dev/docs/orientation/)

### What It Covers

A live session led by experienced contributors that walks through:

- How the Kubernetes project is organized (SIGs, Working Groups, governance)
- How to find your first issue
- The PR workflow (fork, branch, CLA, review, merge)
- Common tools and bots (Prow, Tide, `/assign`, `/lgtm`, `/approve`)
- Where to get help

### Who It's For

- People who have never contributed to Kubernetes
- People who have tried to contribute but got stuck
- Anyone who wants to understand how the project works before diving in

### How to Attend

1. Check the schedule at [kubernetes.dev/docs/orientation/](https://kubernetes.dev/docs/orientation/)
2. Sessions are announced on Slack in `#sig-contribex`
3. Join the video call at the scheduled time
4. No registration required

---

## Self-Paced New Contributor Course

**URL:** [kubernetes.dev/docs/onboarding](https://kubernetes.dev/docs/onboarding)

### What It Is

An online, self-paced course that covers the same material as the live orientation, plus additional depth on:

- Setting up your development environment
- Understanding the Kubernetes codebase structure
- Running tests locally
- The KEP process for those who want to propose features

### When to Use It

- You can't attend the live session
- You want to go at your own pace
- You want to review specific topics after the live session
- You prefer written/interactive content over video

---

## After Orientation

Once you've attended the orientation or completed the course:

1. **Find a good first issue** — [go.k8s.io/good-first-issue](https://go.k8s.io/good-first-issue)
2. **Practice the PR workflow** — [contributor playground](https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/contributor-playground)
3. **Join SIG meetings** — Pick the SIG closest to your interest and attend their regular meeting
4. **Stay connected on Slack** — `#kubernetes-contributors` and `#sig-contribex`
